Code Log 1: HTML Semantic Tags

What are Semantic Tags?

Semantic tags are tags that affect the webpage. They alter and style the page depending on their uses, and their names define what they do. Examples include:

  • <head> - container of title tag
  • <title> - makes text the headline of the page
  • <h1> - styles text to header 1 settings
  • <p> - styles text to paragraph settings
