I'm Luffy! The Man Who Will Become the Pirate King!

Luffy is in a barrell, lost in the sea not knowing where he is.

Luffy wakes up and breaks the barrell, in a ship.

Luffy befriends a guy named Koby who explains to him that he is on a pirate ship, but the captain is none other than the dreadful Captain Alvida.

Koby advises to not mess with Alvida and hide until he can get off the ship but Luffy doesn't understand.
